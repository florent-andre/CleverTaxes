About

Goals

Main goal for this project is to provide to the citizens a simple, comprehensible and participatory access to the budget of organisations, town hall, states,...

For now OpenBudget.fr is a POC, that actually provide :

  • graphical visualization of French state budget data on a scale that mean for the citizen (related to the user's taxes)
  • a way for the citizen to define his ideal budget's repartition
  • a visualization of the average budget's repartition done by others citizens.

A lot of others functionnalities are planned to enhance citizen empoowerment on the budget definition. For example :

  • Get the detail of a budget line on click
  • Offer forums, document management system, and social functionnalities for each budget and/or budget line
  • Allow citizen to propose new budget lines
  • Offer others graphical ways to browse the budget (pie chart, cumulative graph,...) to be more pertinent depending on the user
  • Provide historical and/or prospective budget repartitions
  • Offer comparable views beetween options
  • Make citizen the option to vote for a specific repartition
  • ...

This previous proposals are mostly oriented on the visualization aspects and citizens empowerment, but we also have a lot of ideas to easiest the work of the people in charge of the budget in the organizations : make the organisation's budget worker easier for a beter sharing from the source.
